welcome to our charity

bring water to the kids

Nawiri, we believe that every child deserves access to clean,
safe water. Water is not just a basic necessity; it's a
fundamental right that empowers communities, promotes
health, and supports education. Our mission is to ensure that
children in underserved areas have reliable access to this
precious resource.. - Well Construction: We build wells and
water catchment systems in communities that lack reliable
sources of water. - *Water Purification*: Our purification
programs ensure that the water collected is safe for drinking.

provide good shelter for the poor

Nawiri is dedicated to creating sustainable housing solutions
for families experiencing homelessness or inadequate shelter.
We collaborate with local communities and organizations to
design and implement projects that not only provide immediate
relief but also foster long-term stability and growth.
